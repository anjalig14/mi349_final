The Importance of Food

Anjali G
Oct 30, 2022

A widely stressed upon topic that is arrising is the importance of food. Quoted from Vandana Shiva, an Indian
scholar, environmental activist, food sovereignty advocate, ecofeminist and anti-globalisation, quoted that
"Food is a weapon". This is based on her reasoning that when you control weapons, you control armies. And when
you control food, you control society. But when you control seed you control life on earth. I think that this
is a very important concern that should spark many people's attention. Whether we are aware of it or not, it
is widely noticeable how much control the government and other industries have on the food industry. The chemicals
and pesticides used to make our source of fuel have immensely impacted our health which has possitively contributed
towards the revenue in the pharmaceutical industry. It is being assumed that the pharmaceutical industry and medical
industry aim is to not cure our issues but temper them through drugs by making us come back again when issues arise.
A big contribution to a high amount of ill health is the food and products that we consume. That is why it is
important to understand where our food comes from and how it is harvested.

The Importance of Reading

Anjali G
Oct 30, 2022

Our brains are very malleable. Therefore, it is very important to be able to d ifferentiate right from wrong.
In order to be able to build our intellegence and ability to decipher, we should make an effort to research
and broaden our knowledge and persepctive about certain issues and topics. To do this, books will become your
best friend. It is essential to invest time into reading. No matter what topic you wish to learn more about
whether it be finance, health, spirituality, self growth, books are your way there. There are many secrets and
useful points held in books that are not talked about. Reading not only heightens your knowledge, but it can improve
your communication skills, focus, and memory. It is important to stretch the flexibility of our mind. Due to short
videos that we see on social media platforms, our attention span and retention ability has weakened contributing to
our lack of focus. Not only that but we live in a very intergrated society. More than ever. Through books written
by authors from different time periods and places, there is most likely one person who has gone through the same
experiences as you. And by reading those books, you can seek them for the answers to your questions.
